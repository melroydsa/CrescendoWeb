Crescendo - The music club of SJEC is a subdivision of the cultural committee of SJEC in the musical front. The only club of it's kind that promotes a huge diversity of musical genres and stage performance etiquettes in a unique fashion, for the very first time since the beginning of the institution.

Officially established in ( April 2022 ) the club offers numerous opportunities to the students and staff of the institution to explore their musical side like never before. As of now, our club consists of 40 members, with noteworthy musical skills, that includes vocalists, workstation keyboardists and pianists, electric, acoustic, and bass guitarists, percussionists, and music producers as well.

Our Club is also the mantle for a complete set of musical instruments, and an astounding sound system setup, comprising of state-of-the-art equipments including loudspeakers and mixers / controllers, to name a few. We also collaborate with Melodium, the SJEC studio, for various team oriented projects.

As a team, we thoroughly encourage, and promote active participation of the students in various intercollegiate and intravenous college events, with the aim of equipping them with confindence, elimination of stage fear and limitless exploration and development of their talents in the musical front.
